Core Experience

Cosmic Playground

Command 25+ celestial weapons including singularity drills and quantum missiles. Witness realistic planetary fragmentation physics with debris fields persisting between sessions.

Creative Destruction

Customize planets layer-by-layer before destruction. Earn Stardust Currency through creative demolition to unlock exoplanets and nebula weapons. Advanced weapon modding allows beam width adjustment (5-500px) and explosion delay timing.

Sandbox Freedom

Toggle between preset solar systems or build custom galaxies. The Earth 2100 scenario features climate-altered continents with destructible megacities. Reset systems instantly with Ctrl+R for endless experimentation.
